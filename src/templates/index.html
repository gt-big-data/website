About

We are a student organization of the College Of Computing at Georgia Tech.

We work on projects set by the group related to Big Data. No skill is needed to join the Org, and there are no membership dues.

Make sure to check out our previous projects, sign up to our email list and see what we plan for the 2018-2019 academic year!

Projects


Reddit Comment Communities

Twitter Insights

Our current project. Long term project analyzing the differences in semantics of Tweets between different regions.

Movie Recommendation Website

Still looking for the right movie to watch next? This application will compare your tastes with a database of hundreds of other users to recommend you something new.

Retina News

Web application that organizes news articles to serve them intelligently to the user. Aggregates headlines and articles from a variety of sources, and personalizes delivery of these articles.

Visualizing Pokemon Stats

D3.js visualization of all first generation Pokemon, grouping, graphing and comparing them by their base stats.
